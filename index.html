Game Description

Game on! As a slave sentenced to a life of servitude, you must race your way to freedom in a deadly racing game set in a post-apocalyptic future. But there’s a catch. Your car doesn’t brake nor decelerate. When you reach a certain acceleration level (42V) you activate a certain special ability (power-up), but your car will start to break, so you cannot use for too long. To reduce your acceleration, you can use one of the coolant packs you can collect along the track.

World Setting

In a world that has felt the dire consequences of human greed and mismanagement, humanity has been reduced to small pockets of pseudo anarchic societies beset by the hardships of climate change and resource shortage. Little more than deserts and arctic wastes remain on the planet.

Nitro Powerup

Want to go fast? Nitrous Upgrade is your choice. Your car will burn out faster, but if you want to beat your high score, nitrous is the way to go. Once you go over the limit and activate Nitrous, you car will vastly increase its speed, but be careful, as it will also become much harder to control.

Sludge Powerup

A sturdy solution which allows you to stay ahead, if you get ahead! Once you get over 42v with Sludge installed, you will start dropping sludge on the track which will slow down your adversaries. Even better, your car will become sturdier to the pressure of high V, allowing you to use this upgrade for longer, and ride over the limit for longer.

Tesla Powerup

Are you a mad scientist? No? You’ll still love having a Tesla Coil strapped to the ceiling of your car. Once active, the Tesla fires lightning bolts at your opponents, damaging them constantly, and reducing their engine’s performance over time. Two in one!

Missile Powerup

Of course there are missiles. Go over 42v and start firing a barrage of homing missiles to your opposition. The missiles take their time but eventually will find their target and unleash chaos upon them. Beware, though, you will lose health faster than with most upgrades while over the limit.

Force Push Powerup

Sometimes you just want to push people around. That’s when you pick Force Push. Activate it and drive other cars over the edge of the road, or make sure they can’t go past you as you emit force waves that hold them back. Bonus points: Missiles can't hit you with push active.

No Powerup

Don’t have enough recognition and victories to buy you an upgrade? Just ride it vanilla and try to hold your own. The car is pretty sturdy if you don’t use any upgrades, but you miss out on all the action.
